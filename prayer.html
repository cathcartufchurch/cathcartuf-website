Prayer Times

We meet together as a congregation and with other churches in the area. We have online and in-person praying in groups as well as prayer space events with stations.

Prayer Time for Cathcart UF — Mondays, 8–9pm

A small group meets together on Zoom to pray silently or out loud over things on the world, nationally, locally, for the church and personally.

  • 1st and 3rd Monday — Zoom and in-person at the church
  • Other Mondays — Zoom only

To receive Zoom details please contact us.

Joint Prayer Breakfast with Cathcart Church of Scotland

Held on the 1st Saturday of each month, 8:30–10am at Cathcart United Free Church. We share breakfast together (8:30–9am) followed by a time of prayer together around a theme. All are welcome.
