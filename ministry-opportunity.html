Ministry Opportunity at Cathcart UF

We Are Seeking a Minister

We are seeking a minister to serve on a full-time or part-time basis who will help us in our aims of “Growing together in Christ, serving the community, sharing Jesus’ love” through worship and preaching, pastoral care and working with us in the local community.

For more information please see our profile document.

Anyone seeking a ministry appointment within the United Free Church of Scotland needs to have completed the appropriate application process. Information on this can be obtained from the United Free Church Office:

office@ufcos.org.uk

About Cathcart United Free Church

We are a small, warm and welcoming congregation in the south side of Glasgow. We worship together on Sundays at 11am and meet midweek on Wednesdays at 10:30am for a Time for Reflection.

We work in partnership with Cathcart Church of Scotland and Cathcart Baptist Church on a number of local initiatives, including Radiate Cathcart youth ministry and Renew Wellbeing. Find out more about our partnerships →

If you would like to visit or find out more about our congregation before making contact, you are very welcome to join us on a Sunday morning or get in touch.
