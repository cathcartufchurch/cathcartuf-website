Off the CUF

“Therefore welcome one another as Christ has welcomed you, for the glory of God.”

Romans 15:7

When & Where

When: First Saturday of each month, 10:30am–12pm

Where: Cathcart United Free Church, 7 Struan Road, Glasgow G44 3AT

Cost: Free

For directions and travel information, see our How to Find Us page. If you have any questions, please get in touch.

What is Off the CUF?

Off the CUF is a relaxed and friendly morning gathering that takes place on the first Saturday of every month. It is open to absolutely everyone — whether you are part of our congregation, curious about church, or simply looking for some company and a good brunch.

On the first Saturday of each month we gather in an informal café-style setting to explore a theme that speaks to our shared human experience. We introduce different perspectives, including Christian viewpoints, creating space for open conversation where we can listen, share, and learn from each other.

Topics explored in the past include: Joy, Global aid, Peace, Olympic inspirations, God's love.

There is no programme to follow and no pressure. Come along, grab a drink, have some food, and see where the morning takes you. The three ingredients are simple:

Food

Refuel with hot drinks and a simple brunch. Come hungry!

Friendship

Play a game, bring a hobby, meet new people. There is always something going on and a warm welcome waiting.

Faith

Discuss current topics with personal reflections and a chance to ask questions — no question is too big or too small.

See it for yourself

Spread the Word

Off the CUF is designed to be a place where everyone feels comfortable to bring a friend or neighbour, regardless of their background or beliefs. If you know someone who might enjoy a relaxed Saturday morning out, please do invite them along.
