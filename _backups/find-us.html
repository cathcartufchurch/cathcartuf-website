How to Find Us

We are located at 7 Struan Road, Glasgow G44 3AT in the Cathcart area of Glasgow's south side.

Coming by Car

There is limited on-street parking around the church on Struan Road and Kildare Road. Please be aware that it can be particularly busy for parking when there are events on at the nearby Couper Institute.

We do not have any designated parking for Blue Badge holders.

Coming by Public Transport

By Bus

First Bus services 4 and 6 stop nearby on Clarkston Road, a short walk from the church. Please check the First Bus website for current timetables:

firstgroup.com/greater-glasgow

By Train

Cathcart Railway Station is approximately 400 metres from the church — around a 5-minute walk.

The following ScotRail services stop at Cathcart:

  • The Cathcart Circle Line from Glasgow Central
  • The Neilston Line from Glasgow Central

Please note that the Newton train does not stop at Cathcart.

Check ScotRail timetables at scotrail.co.uk

Joining Us Online

If you are unable to join us in person, our Sunday service is available on Zoom and is livestreamed on our YouTube channel every week.

To receive Zoom details please contact us.
