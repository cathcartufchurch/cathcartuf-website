Events

Here you will find details of special events and activities coming up at Cathcart United Free Church and in our wider community. For our regular weekly meetings please see the Meetings page.

June 2026

Saturday 6th June • 8:30–10am • Cathcart United Free Church

Prayer Breakfast

Our monthly joint Prayer Breakfast with Cathcart Church of Scotland and Cathcart Baptist Church. Breakfast is served from 8:30–9am followed by a time of prayer together around a theme. Topic TBA.

All are welcome.

Saturday 6th June • 10:30am–12pm • Cathcart United Free Church

Off the CUF

Our monthly café-style gathering exploring a theme that speaks to our shared human experience. Food, Friendship and Faith — free and open to all. Children welcome, under the responsibility of a parent or carer.

Sunday 28th June • 11am • Cathcart United Free Church

Love Your Church — Sunday Service

We continue our series looking at the early church in Acts 1–12. This Sunday we will be exploring the second chapter of the book Love Your Church by Tony Merida — what it means to be a welcoming church.

Over the coming months we are encouraging people to read the book and discuss it with others. Once a month in the Sunday morning service we'll take up one of the eight themes:

  • Belonging (26th April) ✓
  • Welcoming (31st May) ✓
  • Gathering
  • Caring
  • Service
  • Honouring
  • Witnessing
  • Sending
