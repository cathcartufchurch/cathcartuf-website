Meetings

We would love to see you at any of our gatherings. Whether you are a regular churchgoer or simply curious, you are very welcome at Cathcart United Free Church.

Sunday at 11

We meet together in the church and on Zoom every Sunday from 11:00am to 12:15pm. Our Sunday gathering is the heart of our week together — a time to worship God, explore the Bible, pray and sing. Whether you are joining us in person or online, you are warmly welcome.

After the service there is tea and coffee, and plenty of opportunity to chat and get to know each other. We are a friendly congregation and nobody need feel they are on their own.

Join us on Zoom or YouTube

We also livestream our Sunday service on our YouTube channel. To join us on Zoom please contact us by email and we will send you the details.

Midweek — Time for Reflection

Every Wednesday at 10:30am we gather in the church building for a quiet 40-minute reflective time together. This is a gentler, more contemplative gathering — a space to pause mid-week and reconnect with God.

Each session includes a Bible reading, a short talk and music. There is no Zoom option for this gathering — it is purely in person, which adds to its intimate and unhurried character. All are welcome.
