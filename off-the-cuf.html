Off the CUF

First Saturday of the month, 10:30am–12pm

Held at Cathcart United Free Church, 7 Struan Road, Glasgow

All welcome — any children will be the responsibility of a parent or carer.

Cost: Free!

Food

Refuel with hot drinks and a simple brunch.

Friendship

Play a game, bring a hobby, meet new people.

Faith

Discuss current topics with personal reflections and a chance to ask questions.

About Off the CUF

On the first Saturday of each month we gather in an informal café-style setting to explore a theme that speaks to our shared human experience. We introduce different perspectives, including Christian viewpoints, creating space for open conversation where we can listen, share, and learn from each other.

Topics explored in the past include: Joy, Global aid, Peace, Olympic inspirations, God's love.
