Welcome to Cathcart United Free Church

We are a warm and welcoming congregation based in the Cathcart area of Glasgow. Whether you are a long-standing member or visiting for the first time, you are very welcome here.

We meet every Sunday for worship and throughout the week for a variety of activities and community events. Please explore our site to find out more about who we are and what we do.
